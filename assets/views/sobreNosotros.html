SUSTAINABILITY IN OUR FOOD TRUCKS

Since we started serving delicious food from our food truck, we have focused on reducing our CO2 footprint. In all our business choices, interior design of food trucks and choice of raw materials, we therefore take into account how sustainable this is in relation to the alternative. That is why the FILL ME UP! EMPANADAS FOOD TRUCK runs exclusively on electricity , so we avoid being dependent on gas or diesel generators. In addition, we only use recyclable metal plates , which we take with us and wash when we drive from an event - this way we make sure that our food truck does not waste unnecessary resources and creates as little rubbish as possible. Two of our empanadas are vegetarian and we are constantly working on how we can use as few animal products as possible to reduce our CO2 footprint.

ALLERGENS & DIETS

Our menu is composed in a way that everyone can eat with. Three options in our menu are vegetarian and can be served vegan . All empanadas can be served lactose free and we have the option of gluten free empanadas. This means that when you are visited by the FILL ME UP! EMPANADAS FOOD TRUCK for your party, you are sure that all your guests will be full and can eat with our delicious empanadas menu.
