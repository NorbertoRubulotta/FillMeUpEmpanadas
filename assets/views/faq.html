FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Menu

+ What kind of food do you serve?

We serve five different empanadas from our food truck, which you can read more about here . You can find reviews of the experience and the food here.

+ How fast and how does the serving take place?

All the guests at your event will receive a plate with the three different empanadas of their choise. At an event of 50 people, everyone typically got the first round within 30 minutes. Afterwards, guests can come up and get more empanadas until they are full.

+ Do you have vegetarian and vegan alternatives?

Yes! Three of our empanadas are vegetarian and they can also be served vegan.

Service

+ Is there anything I need to prepare?

The only thing you need to prepare before we arrive is that our food truck has access to a standard power plug within 30 meters. Other than that, all you have to do is enjoy your event.

+ Do I have to provide cutlery and plates?

No. We have everything we need so you do not have to worry about it.

+ What is the size of your food truck?

Our food trucks are 3.4m long , 2.4m high and 1.55m wide .

+ When do you arrive in?

We arrive no later than 30 minutes before the agreed serving time. Sometimes we set up the food truck a few hours before to make it fit with the rest of the logistics for our other food trucks.

+ How long will you stay?

Our food truck basically serves out with you for 1.5 hours for smaller events. If you have a larger event or a type of event that requires longer serving, we can arrange this in more detail. Feel free to fill out the form on this page with your wishes.
